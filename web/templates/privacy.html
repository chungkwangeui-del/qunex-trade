Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

Qunex Trade ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information:

  • Email address
  • Username
  • Password (encrypted)
  • Account preferences

Usage Data:

  • IP address
  • Browser type
  • Pages visited
  • Time and date of visits
  • Feature usage patterns

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Service
  • Send service notifications and updates
  • Improve our AI models and features
  • Prevent fraud and abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Sharing

We will NEVER sell your personal data or share your trading activity with third parties for marketing purposes.

We may share data with:

  • Analytics Services: Google Analytics (anonymized)
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law

5. Data Security

We implement security measures including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all connections
  • Password hashing with industry-standard algorithms
  • Secure database storage
  • Regular security updates
  • Access controls and monitoring

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data
  • Export: Download your data
  • Withdraw Consent: Delete your account anytime

7. Cookies

We use cookies for:

  • Session management (login status)
  • Analytics (Google Analytics)
  • Preferences (theme settings)

You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

8. Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained while account is active
  • Deleted Accounts: Data purged within 30 days

9. Children's Privacy

Qunex Trade is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or website notice.

11. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or to exercise your rights, contact:
Email: privacy@qunextrade.com
