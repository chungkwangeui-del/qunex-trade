Core Features

1. AI-Powered News Analysis

Our AI system (Claude 3 Haiku) analyzes every news article in real-time, filtering out analyst opinions, speculation, and promotional content. Only factual events make it to your dashboard.

  • Intelligent Filtering: Automatically removes "should you buy", "analyst predicts", and speculative content
  • Real Events Only: Fed announcements, economic data releases, actual policy changes‚Äînot predictions
  • Market-Wide vs. Specific: Instantly identifies news affecting entire markets (Fed, Trump policies) vs. individual stocks
  • Importance Scoring: 5-star rating system prioritizes critical market-moving events

2. Multi-Source News Collection

We aggregate news from the most credible financial sources to ensure you never miss critical market information.

  • Polygon.io: Real-time market news with verified accuracy
  • Tier-1 Financial Sources: Bloomberg, Reuters, WSJ, Financial Times, CNBC, MarketWatch
  • Automatic Deduplication: No repetitive news across sources
  • Hourly Updates: Fresh analysis every hour, all day long

3. Professional Dashboard

Clean, distraction-free interface designed for traders who need information fast.

  • Filter by Importance: View all news, 5-star critical, or 4-star important events
  • Market-Wide First: News affecting entire markets appears at the top
  • Real-Time Refresh: One-click update to get latest news
  • Responsive Design: Works perfectly on desktop, tablet, and mobile
