## Trading Strategies by Event Type

### Strategy 1: **Straddle (Before Major Events)**
- **When**: 15 minutes before FOMC, NFP, CPI
- **How**: Place buy stop above resistance AND sell stop below support
- **Why**: Catches explosive move in either direction
- **Risk**: False breakouts

### Strategy 2: **Fade the Spike (After Release)**
- **When**: Immediately after extreme reaction
- **How**: Trade against the initial spike
- **Why**: Markets often overreact then reverse
- **Risk**: Trend may continue

### Strategy 3: **Trade the Trend (30-60 min After)**
- **When**: After initial volatility settles
- **How**: Identify new trend direction, enter on pullback
- **Why**: Cleanest trend trading opportunity
- **Risk**: Trend may be exhausted

### Strategy 4: **Avoid Trading (Safest)**
- **When**: 30 min before to 30 min after high-impact events
- **Why**: Extreme volatility, wide spreads, unpredictable moves
- **Best for**: Beginners and capital preservation

### 2. **Whisper Numbers**
